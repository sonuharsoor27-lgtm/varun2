Key Train Seat Information & Policies
•	Availability: Check real-time availability for 1A, 2A, 3A, CC, SL, and 2S classes before booking.
•	Current Reservation: Charts are generally prepared 4 hours before departure, with final booking options closing 30 minutes before departure.
•	Seat Confirmation (CNF): A 'CNF' status means your seat/berth is secured.
•	Waiting List (WL) & RAC: If a ticket is booked under RAC, a seat is provided, but it may be shared. WL tickets must be confirmed before boarding.
•	Children Policy: Children under 5 travel free (no seat), while those aged 5-12 pay full fare if a berth is booked, or half-fare otherwise.
•	ID Requirement: A valid photo ID (Aadhaar, Voter ID, License) is mandatory for travel.
